PGDrive: A highly flexible open-ended driving simulator

1The Chinese University of Hong Kong

Overview
To better evaluate and improve the generalization of end-to-end driving, we introduce an open-ended and highly configurable driving simulator called PGDrive. PGDrive first defines multiple basic road blocks such as ramp, fork, and roundabout with configurable settings. Then a range of diverse maps can be assembled from those blocks with procedural generation, which are further turned into interactive environments.
